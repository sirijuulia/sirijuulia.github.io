About Me

I am a dabbler in creative pursuits. I use creativity to make sense of the world, to pay attention, to find and use my voice in a noisy world. At the moment, I mostly write and take photos and work on creative recovery. I also enjoy trying my hand in crafts that I’m woefully rubbish at, such as pottery and modern dance. Being bad at things brings me great joy.

I’ve set up this website to record my thoughts, my creations, my process – I’m feeling very much at a start of a journey but it feels important to be present in the world from the beginning.

You can also find my photos on Facebook at Siri Juulia Photography; and my poems on Instagram at @Crossroadspoetry.
